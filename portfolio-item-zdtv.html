Zions Direct Google TV

October 2011 ||| Role: Application Developer|||Press Article

This is an android google tv application that delivers the latest investment news, educational vidos, and auctions info.

This app combines web browseing experience and tv viewing experience in one. We achieved that by provideing the users the access to all of the content in two modes: app mode and auto mode. The app mode is suited for an active exploration, similar to searching for content online. In the auto mode, the app displays the video, news, and auction info one item at the time to mimic the tv viewing experience.
